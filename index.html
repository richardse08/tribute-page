A Brief History of the Colonel
  1. Colonel Harland Sanders, better known as "The Colonel", was born on Tuesday September 9, 1890, in Henryville, Indiana
  2. At age 5, his father passed away
  3. At age 16, he dropped out of school
  4. By age 17, he had already been employed, and terminated, four times
  5. When he was merely 20 years old, his wife left him and took the baby leaving Harland alone
  6. Over the years, The Colonel worked as a farmer, streetcar conductor, soldier, lawyer, insurance salesman, steamboat operator, secretary, lighting manufacturer and many others
  7. Despite his multitude of odd-jobs, The Colonel worked mostly as a cook until his eventual retirement at 65
  8. Upon realzing his life was a failure, Colonel Harland Sanders decided he would end it and take his own life...
  9. While writing his will, The Colonel thought to himself what he could have accomplished with his life had he done things differently
  10. It became clear to the elderly Harland that he only excelled at one thing: Cooking
  11. In a true leap of faith, Colonel Harland Sanders started selling fried chicken door to door
  12. By age 88, the founder of the Kentucky Fried Chicken Franchise was a Billionaire...
